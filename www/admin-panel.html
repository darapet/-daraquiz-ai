🔐 How to Access This Panel

Bookmark this page: admin-panel.html — or navigate to it from the sidebar on admin pages.

You must be signed in with an email address that has admin privileges in Firestore. Your admin account is the one configured in the settings/admin document.

If you are locked out, go to Firebase Console → Firestore → settings → admin and add your email to the admin_emails array.
